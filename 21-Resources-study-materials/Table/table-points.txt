Points for Table structure:

1) styling k liyey internal or inline lagay gi bus.
2) font family sirf : font-family: arial, sans-serif;
3) image par display: block lagay ga.
4) margin ki property nai lagti is may, padding b table data (td)  par hi lagay gi bus. 
5) table row (tr) par style attribute k zariyey height lagay gi. e.g <tr style="height: 200px;">
6) table data (td) par style attribute k zariyey width lagay gi. e.g <td style="width: 300px;">

7) (td) ki column-spaning k liyey attribute "colspan" . e.g: colspan="2"
	matlab yey hay k "2 <td>" expand hojae ga.
     => jis "td" par columns-spaning karni hogi , iski column-spaning apny parent k siblings "tr"s  ki maimum "td"(columns)
	 (quantity)  par depend kary gi. 
	
8) (td) ki row-spaning k liyey attribute "rowspan". e.g:  rowspan="2"
	=> jis "td" par row-spaning karni hogi , iski row-spaning is k siblings (td)s  ki  "tr"s   ki maimum "tr"s(quantity)  par
        depend kary gi.

9) aam torh par styling <td> par hoti hay.

10) <td>  aik tarhan ka data container hota hay, is k childs "html elements", "text", "images", "lists", or "nested tables"
    b ho sakhtay hay.

11) table ko samajnay k liyey , ye hay k "tr" ko "flex container" samjah jae or "tds" ko is k flex items.
